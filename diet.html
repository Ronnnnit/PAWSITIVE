Dog Diet

Labrador Retriever: Feed a diet suitable for large breeds, focusing on lean proteins and moderate portions to avoid obesity.
Golden Retriever: Offer a balanced diet with fresh fruits and vegetables as treats, while monitoring calorie intake to prevent obesity.
German Shepherd: Provide easily digestible proteins and supplements for joint health, with controlled portions to prevent digestive issues.
Indian Pariah Dog: Feed a diet for medium-sized breeds, incorporating varied protein sources and monitoring portions to prevent obesity.
Pomeranian: Offer small, frequent meals of high-quality food for small breeds, with dental chews for oral health.

Cat Diet

Persian Cat: Feed a diet tailored for hairball control and skin health, ensuring hydration with wet food or water-added dry food.
Siamese Cat: Provide a protein-rich diet to support their high energy levels, with interactive feeding toys for mental stimulation.
Maine Coon Cat: Offer a diet formulated for large breeds, rich in protein and essential fatty acids, with supplements for overall health and mobility.
Indian Shorthair Cat: Feed a balanced diet with varied protein sources, monitoring portions to prevent weight issues.
Bengal Cat: Opt for a protein-rich diet to match their active lifestyle, supplemented with interactive toys for mental stimulation.
